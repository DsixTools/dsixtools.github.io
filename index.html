DsixTools is a Mathematica package for the matching
and renormalization-group evolution from the New
Physics scale to the scale of low energy observables.


The current version of DsixTools includes:
  • Three-loop SM RGEs, and five-loop QCD corrections to the running of the strong and quark Yukawa couplings
  • Full one-loop RGEs in the SMEFT
  • Repository of analytical expressions for one loop beta functions and matching conditions in Mathematica format
  • One-loop matching between the SMEFT and the LEFT at the electroweak scale
  • Full one-loop QCD and QED running from the electroweak scale down to the scale of B physics
  • Comprehensive reference and documentation environment
