DsixTools is a Mathematica package for the matching
and renormalization-group evolution from the New
Physics scale to the scale of low energy observables.


The current version of DsixTools includes:
  • SMEFTrunner: Full one-loop renormalization-group evolution in the Standard Model Effective Field Theory.
  • EWmatcher: Tree-level matching of the SMEFT to the Weak Effective Theory at the electroweak scale.
  • WETrunner: Full one-loop QCD and QED running from the electroweak scale down to the scale of B physics.
